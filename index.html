Join our Server

EnduraCraft is a semi vanilla Minecraft Survival server with quality of life plugins to make your experience even better! You can play with your friends, make land claims, make towns, make player warps, buy & sell items to others and more!

Join us in just

Three steps

You will need Minecraft Java Edition - 1.17-1.18.

  • Launch Minecraft, click on Multiplayer,

  • Click on Add Server,

  • Use play.enduracraft.xyz as the IP, and
    enable the Server Resource Packs.

You're all set. Start your fantastic journey today.
